Pizza

DESCRIPTION

Pizza is an Italian dish made with a thin dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. To start, you prepare a soft yeast dough using flour, water, yeast, sugar, salt, and olive oil, then let it rise. Once the dough becomes fluffy, it’s rolled out into a circle, spread with tomato sauce (usually made with garlic and herbs), and sprinkled with grated cheese.

Next, you add your favorite toppings — such as pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, bell peppers, or other vegetables and meats. The pizza is then baked in the oven at 200–220°C (about 400–430°F) for 15–20 minutes until the crust turns golden and the cheese melts. Once ready, the pizza is sliced and served hot.

INGREDIENTS

For the dough:

  • All-purpose flour – 250-300g
  • Warm water – 250ml
  • Dry yeast – 1 teaspoon
  • Sugar – 1 teaspoon
  • Salt – 1 teaspoon
  • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons

For the toppings

  • Shredded mozzarella cheese – 150–200 g
  • Sausage or pepperoni – 100 g
  • Mushrooms – 4–5 pieces
  • Olives or black olives – optional

For the sause

  • Tomato paste or crushed tomatoes – 4 tablespoons
  • Garlic – 1–2 cloves
  • Sugar – 1/2 teaspoon

STEPS

  1. Make the dough:

    Preheat your oven to In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until it becomes foamy.

  2. Add flour and salt:

    Gradually add flour and salt to the yeast mixture. Add olive oil and knead the dough for about 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic

  3. Let the dough rise:

    Cover the bowl with a towel or plastic wrap and let the dough rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until it doubles in size.

  4. Prepare the sauce:

    In a small bowl, mix tomato paste (or crushed tomatoes) with garlic, olive oil, salt, sugar, and herbs. Stir well.

  5. Preheat the oven

    Preheat your oven to 200–220°C

  6. Roll out the dought

    Once the dough has risen, roll it out on a floured surface into a circle or rectangle, depending on your tray.

  7. Add sauce and toppings:

    Spread the sauce over the dough, add shredded mozzarella, and your favorite toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers).

  8. Bake the pizza

    Place the pizza on a baking tray or pizza stone and bake for 15–20 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.

  9. Serve
