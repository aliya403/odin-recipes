Salad

DESCRIPTION

Vegetable salad is a quick and healthy dish, perfect as a side or a light meal. To make it, chop fresh cucumbers, tomatoes, and bell peppers into bite-sized pieces. Add thinly sliced red onion and a handful of lettuce or mixed greens. For extra flavor and texture, you can also include black olives or crumbled feta cheese.

In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice (or vinegar), a pinch of salt, and black pepper to make a simple dressing. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss everything gently. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to absorb the flavors, then serve immediately. It's refreshing, colorful, and full of vitamins

INGREDIENTS

For the dough:

  • Cucumbers – 2
  • Tomatoes – 2–3
  • Bell pepper – 1
  • Red onion – 1-2
  • Lettuce or mixed greens – about 30-40g
  • Olives or black olives – optional

For the dressing

  • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons
  • Salt – to taste
  • Lemon juice or wine vinegar – 1 tablespoon

STEPS

  1. Wash the vegetables:

    Rinse the cucumbers, tomatoes, bell pepper, red onion, and lettuce thoroughly under running water.

  2. Chop the ingredients:

    Cut cucumbers, tomatoes, and bell pepper into bite-sized pieces. Thinly slice the red onion. Tear the lettuce into smaller pieces.

  3. Combine in a bowl:

    Place all the chopped vegetables into a large salad bowl.

  4. Prepare the dressing:

    In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice (or vinegar), salt, and black pepper. Stir well.

  5. Dress the salad:

    Pour the dressing over the vegetables and gently toss until everything is evenly coated.

  6. Let it sit

    Let the salad rest for 5–10 minutes so the flavors can blend.
